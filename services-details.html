EXTERIOR DESIGN

Architectural planning

An office space must capture the essence of a company and provide employees with a sense of belonging and identity. It’s about the big decision, but also the small details that define a place. These things, combined with a meticulously planned design.

We make art with light. We create large-scale luminous and digital artworks for architectural facades, city precincts, commercial spaces. We combine light, sculpture, digital media and interactive technology to create works for architectural projects and public spaces. We believe great works are born from respectful and collaborative relationships. We also believe that the inspiration already exists within a project and our job is to unveil it.We also have a passion for 3D, especially environment art and hard surface modeling. We are proficient with many different tools.
